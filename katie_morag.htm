Katie Morag

Mairi Hedderwick

Katie Morag McColl lives on the island of Struay, in Scotland. Her adventures are adventurous, and never sacharine or preachy.

  • Katie Morag and the Grand Concert by Mairi Hedderwick. When trouble threatens the annual Struay Grand Concert, Katie comes through. (ages 4 to 6)
  • Katie Morag and the Tiresome Ted by Mairi Hedderwick. In a fit of pique, Katie throws away her beloved teddy bear, then misses him terribly and is very happy when she recovers him. (ages 4 to 6)
  • Katie Morag and the Riddles by Mairi Hedderwick. Katie solves four riddles, and listeners will be proud to solve them, too. (ages 4 to 6)
  • Katie Morag and the Big Boy Cousins by Mairi Hedderwick. When her big boy cousins come to visit, Katie joins them in mischief, but has to pay the price. (ages 4 to 6)
  • Katie Morag and the Two Grandmothers by Mairi Hedderwick. Katie's two grandmothers disagree with each other, but end on better terms. (ages 4 to 6) (out-of-print, see sources.)
  • The Big Katie Morag Storybook by Mairi Hedderwick. A collection of short Katie Morag stories, essays and activities. (ages 4 to 6)
  • Second Katie Morag Storybook by Mairi Hedderwick. A second collection of short Katie Morag stories, essays and activities. (ages 4 to 6)
  • Katie Morags Rainy Day Book by Mairi Hedderwick. Activities to play inside, based on the Katie Morag stories. (ages 4 to 6)
  • Katie Morag Delivers the Mail by Mairi Hedderwick. (ages 4 to 6) (out-of-print, see sources.)
  • Katie Morag and the New Pier by Mairi Hedderwick. (ages 4 to 6) (out-of-print, see sources.)
  • Katie Morag and the Wedding by Mairi Hedderwick. (ages 4 to 6) (out-of-print, see sources.)
