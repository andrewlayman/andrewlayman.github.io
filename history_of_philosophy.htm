History of Philosophy

Philosophy is the science that studies the fundamental nature of existence, of man and of man's relation to existence.

by Leonard Peikoff

These recorded lectures trace the essentials of philosophy's development from its rise in ancient Greece, through Christianity, the Renaissance, the Enlightenment, its decline after Kant and its rebirth after Rand. Published in two volumes.
