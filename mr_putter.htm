Mr Putter

Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator

Mr. Putter and his fine cat, Tabby, have charming, elderly adventures.

  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Pour the Tea by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Mr. Putter, who is not young, finds a cat, who is also not young, to share his adventures. The first of many Mr. Putter and Tabby books, all good. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Bake the Cake by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Mr. Putter and his fine cat, Tabby, after many tries, bake an excellent cake. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Walk the Dog by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Mr. Putter and his fine cat, Tabby, take on the job of walking his neighbor's good dog, Zeke. This turns out to be a difficult job until Mr. Putter rewards good behavior. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Walk the Dog by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Winsome story of old Mr. Putter, his old cat Tabby and the difficulties of walking a neighbor's energetic dog. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Pick the Pears by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Mr. Putter sets out to pick pears, and does so in an unconventional way, resulting in an apple pie. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Row the Boat by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. On a hot, summer day, Mr. Putter and his fine cat, Tabby, have adventures in a boat with their neighbor's, Mrs. Teaberry and her good dog, Zeke. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Paint the Porch by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Mr. Putter decides to paint his porch, but when his fine cat, Tabby, and his neighbor's good dog, Zeke, help out, not all goes right. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Take the Train by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Mr. Putter, his fine cat, Tabby, his neighbor, Mrs Teaberry, and her good dog, Zeke, have not been on a train ride in years. So, when they attempt to take one, they find that no pets are allowed. This obstacle is surmounted. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Mr. Putter and Tabby Feed the Fish by Cynthia Rylant, author; Arthur Howard, illustrator. Mr. Putter buys a fish to entertain himself and his fine cat, Tabby, but the effect is not relaxing. (ages 3 to 4)
