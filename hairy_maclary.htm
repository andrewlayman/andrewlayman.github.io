Hairy Maclary

Lynley Dodd

Jaunty rhymes and engaging stories of Hairy Maclairy, the Scottish Terrier, and his canine compatriots.

  • Hairy Maclary from Donaldson's Dairy by Lynley Dodd. A jaunty little dog and a jaunty little rhyme, Hairy Maclary and an increasing number of friends investigate a new sound. This is the first of a long series of books, all excellent. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Hairy Maclary's Caterwaul Caper by Lynley Dodd. A jaunty rhyme about Hairy Maclairy, his dog friends and a loud cat up a tree. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Hairy Maclary and Zachary Quack by Lynley Dodd. Hairy Maclary tries to escape a playful duckling and in the end, becomes friends. (ages 3 to 4)
  • Hairy Maclary's Bone by Lynley Dodd. Hairy Maclary finds a bone but all the other dogs want it and give chase. Hairy Maclary leads them on a path that one-by-one culls his pursuers. (ages 3 to 4)
